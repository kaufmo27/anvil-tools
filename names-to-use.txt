------------------------------------------------
Anvil Tools: Working Names
------------------------------------------------
-Here you will find all the names for items to change their appearance.
-When renaming in the game, ignore the "+" signs or the quotation marks to make everything work correctly, for example: Ender Sword, Axe of Honor
------------------------------------------------
